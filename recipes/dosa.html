Dosa

Description

Dosa is a traditional South Indian dish known for it thiness and crispiness. Dosas are typically served hot with various accompaniments like coconut chutney, sambar, and potato masala filling. However, Dosas can be enjoyed either plain or stuffed with different fillings, making them a staple in South Indian cuisine and a popular breakfast or snack choice across India.

  3. Add 1 tbsp on a pan over medium heat
